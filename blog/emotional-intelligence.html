Emotional Intelligence

  • What is Emotional Intelligence?

    • Emotional Intelligence (EQ or Emotional Quotient) is being aware of emotions, understanding them, and being able to deal with them in a healthy way. It is not just your own emotions but also the emotions of others. It involves being able to see what others are feeling and why. And using that knowledge to base how you interact with them.

  • How is EQ different from IQ?

    • IQ measures ones ability to problem-solve and think logically. EQ is the ability to understand and manage emotions.

  • Why is EQ important?

    • EQ is an important set of career skills and it helps build stronger relationships.
